Father (2020)

The movie is about a person who is having dementia and how it impacts his daugher's life. However, the more impact the dementia is on his life and the movie depicts how a person feels when he can not make sense of what he is feeling even though he is trying his best to make sense of things.

The director's approach is very unique and is very dramatic style owing to his previous experience in directing plays. I remember the music that comes before dwelling into each separate scene before the father (Anthony Hopkins) removes the headphones or plug off the disk, is very immersive and takes us to journey of an individual who is mercy at not his daughter but to his own memories of his two daughters and his ownself.

The repeatative dailogues in some of the scenes captures the viewer what an individual feels while observing simmilar situations and how ones tries his best to keep his mind sane.

Anthony Hopkins, when he laughs full-heartedly in some of the scenes is remiscinent of his old self and then the final cry in the room of the hospital defines, how his life has ended up in sorry state. And yet he starts to live every morning till the world feels sane.
